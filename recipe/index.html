Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • oil
  • sugar

Process

To make gulab jamun at home, you'll need milk powder, flour, ghee, and sugar. First, mix 1 cup milk powder, 5 tbsp flour, and 1 tsp ghee. Add 2-4 tbsp milk and 1 tbsp yogurt to form a dough. Divide into 14-18 portions and fry until golden. Soak in sugar syrup made with 1.25 cups sugar, 1.5 cups water, and rose water

Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodkes
  • Sauce
  • vegetables
  • oil

Process

Mix 2 cups flour, 2 eggs, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1 tbsp oil. Knead 10 minutes, rest 30 minutes. Roll out to 1/16 inch thickness. Cut into noodle shape. Cook in boiling water 2-3 minutes. Drain, serve with favorite sauce.

Momos

Ingredients

  • flour
  • vegetables
  • oil

Process

Mix 2 cups flour, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp baking powder. Add 1/4 cup ghee and 1/2 cup lukewarm water. Knead dough. Fill with mixture of ground meat, onions, garlic, and spices. Steam or fry until cooked. Serve with chutney
